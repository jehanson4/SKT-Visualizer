The Science Behind the Pictures

Q: What am I looking at?

A: You are looking at the configuration space of an N-dimensional SK model in which the energy surface consists of two isotropic potential wells centered on two distinct points.

Just four numbers are needed to define the fundamental properties of the model:

  • N, its dimensionality
  • k0, the distance between the two points, themselves labeled p1 and p2
  • α1, the depth of the potential well centered on p1
  • α2, the depth of the potential well centered on p2
The thermodynamic properties also depend on:
  • T, temperature (often expressed in terms of its inverse 1/T, denoted β)

Q: What am I looking at?

A: You are looking at an N-dimensional SK model embedded in a hemispherical shell. The model's energy surface consists of two isotropic potential wells centered on two distinct points p1 and p2. Locations on the hemispherical shell represent equivalence classes of the points in the SK model's configuration space: all points in a given equivalence class are at a distance k1 from p1 and k2 from p2. The two equivalence classes respectively containing p1 and p2 themselves are located on the shell's equator.
